## Tips for Personalizing

### Required Replacements
- `[YOUR-URL-HERE]` - Replace with your actual Habitat Builder URL
- `[Name]` - Use their actual name (email only)
- `[their specialty species]` - Mention animals they actually work with
- `[specific thing]` - Reference real content ("your bioactive builds", "your care videos", etc.)

### Best Practices
- **For YouTube Creators:** Use email template, check About section for business email
- **For Instagram/TikTok:** Use DM template
- **For Reptile Shops/Rescues:** Use email template, mention their customers/adopters
- **For Reddit/Facebook:** Use community post templates
- **For Discord:** Use DM template in #general or #introductions channel

### Tone Guidelines
- Lead with value (what the tool does)
- Ask for honest feedback, not endorsements
- Acknowledge their expertise
- Make it clear it's free/no strings attached
- Keep under 150 words for DMs, under 200 for emails
- Specific call-to-action (test X species, takes Y minutes)

### Follow-Up Strategy
If they respond with feedback:
1. Thank them genuinely
2. Ask if they'd like to be credited in acknowledgments
3. If feedback is substantial, offer to add them to a "Reviewed by" section
4. If they share it, thank them publicly (tag them if appropriate)

## Where to Find Contacts

### YouTube Creators
- Search: "reptile care guide", "[species] setup", "[species] enclosure"
- Target: 10k-100k subscriber channels (more likely to respond)
- Contact: Business email in About section

### Instagram/TikTok
- Search hashtags: #reptilesofinstagram #herpkeeper #[species]keeper
- Look for educational content, not just pet photos
- DM directly through the app

### Reddit Communities
- r/reptiles
- r/herpetoculture
- r/[SpecificAnimal] (e.g., r/BeardedDragons, r/frogs)
- Message moderators or post directly

### Facebook Groups
- Search: "reptile keepers [your region]"
- "[Species] owners" groups
- Message admins for permission to post

### Discord Servers
- Search: "reptile discord" or "[species] discord"
- Introduce yourself in general channels
- Ask permission before sharing links

### Organizations
- Local reptile rescues and sanctuaries
- State herpetological societies
- Pet stores specializing in reptiles (not chains)
- Zoo education departments with reptile exhibits
