# Habitat Builder: Project Overview

## What is Habitat Builder?

Habitat Builder is a free web application that helps reptile and amphibian keepers design proper enclosures with science-backed care parameters and equipment recommendations. Think of it as a "habitat design calculator" that takes the guesswork out of setting up an enclosure.

## The Problem We're Solving

Setting up a reptile or amphibian enclosure is **overwhelming** for beginners:

- **Conflicting information**: Forums, YouTube, and pet stores give contradictory advice
- **Equipment confusion**: UVB bulbs, heat sources, substratesâ€”what do you actually need?
- **Size misconceptions**: "20 gallon tanks are fine for bearded dragons" (they're not)
- **Budget surprises**: People don't realize proper setups cost $300-800 until items are in their cart
- **Information overload**: Spending 10+ hours researching care guides across dozens of sources

**Result:** Many animals live in inadequate conditions, not because keepers don't care, but because they didn't know better when starting out.

---

## Our Solution

Habitat Builder provides **instant, customized enclosure plans** in 3 steps:

1. **Select your animal** (White's Tree Frog, Bearded Dragon, etc.)
2. **Enter enclosure dimensions** (or choose from common preset sizes)
3. **Get a complete plan** with:
   - Equipment recommendations (UVB, heating, substrate, decor)
   - Shopping list with budget tiers (low/mid/premium)
   - Quantity calculations (substrate depth, fixture sizing)
   - Step-by-step build instructions
   - Care guidance (feeding, misting, temperature zones)
   - Safety warnings and beginner tips

### What Makes It Different

- **Species-specific**: Not generic "reptile care"â€”tailored to each animal's needs
- **Deterministic calculations**: Equipment sizing based on enclosure volume, temperature differentials, and coverage requirements (not vague AI suggestions)
- **Budget-conscious**: Shows multiple price points so keepers can plan expenses
- **Educational**: Explains *why* certain equipment is needed
- **Completely free**: No paywalls, no accounts required (currently)

## Technology & Approach

### Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: React 18 + TypeScript + Vite
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS (with dark mode)
- **Data**: JSON-based animal profiles (easy to expand)
- **Hosting**: [Your hosting platform]

### Data Sources
All care parameters come from:
- **ReptiFiles** (research-backed care guides by Jessica Archibald)
- **Equipment manufacturers** (official specs for UVB output, wattage)
- **Scientific literature** (thermal biology, habitat studies)
- **Experienced breeders** (real-world validation)

### Why Not AI-Generated?
We use **deterministic formulas**, not AI, because:
- Equipment sizing requires exact calculations (UVB coverage %, substrate volume)
- Consistency mattersâ€”same inputs always give same outputs
- Accountabilityâ€”we can cite sources for every recommendation
- Trustâ€”users know recommendations aren't "hallucinated"
