Generic equipment (water, feeding, humidity-aids, decor) is processed
by dedicated functions (addWaterSupplies, addFeedingSupplies, etc.).

To validate shopping list generation:
1. Generate a plan for each animal
2. Check that all items listed here appear in the shopping list
3. Verify quantities and sizing calculations are appropriate
